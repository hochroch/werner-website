Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about charter bus rental in the Philadelphia area.

How much does it cost to rent a charter bus in Philadelphia?

Charter bus pricing depends on the trip distance, duration, and time of year. A typical single-day charter in the Philadelphia area starts around $1,200 to $1,800 for a full-size 54-passenger motorcoach. Multi-day trips, peak season dates, and longer distances will affect pricing. Contact us at (800) 532-9800 or request a free quote for an exact price based on your group's needs.

How many passengers can a Werner Coach motorcoach hold?

We operate full-size luxury motorcoaches that seat up to 54 passengers. Each coach includes reclining seats, a restroom, climate control, Wi-Fi, power outlets, and a video system. For groups larger than 54, we can provide multiple coaches.

What areas does Werner Coach serve?

Werner Coach is based in Phoenixville, PA, minutes from Philadelphia, Valley Forge, and King of Prussia. We primarily serve the Greater Philadelphia area and southeastern Pennsylvania, and we regularly travel throughout the tri-state region (Pennsylvania, New Jersey, Delaware). Our standard service range covers the entire Northeast corridor from New York City to Washington, D.C., including Baltimore, Atlantic City, the Poconos, and destinations across the East Coast.

How far in advance should I book a charter bus?

We recommend booking at least 2 to 4 weeks in advance for most trips, and 2 to 3 months ahead for peak season (May through October) and popular event dates like prom season and graduation weekends. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible depending on fleet availability. Call (610) 933-7100 to check.

Are your drivers professionally licensed?

Yes. All Werner Coach drivers hold valid Commercial Driver's Licenses (CDL) with passenger endorsements, undergo DOT-mandated background checks and drug testing, and complete ongoing safety training. We maintain full USDOT and FMCSA compliance with regular vehicle inspections and driver qualification file management.

Can I rent a charter bus for a wedding?

Absolutely. Wedding transportation is one of our most popular services. We provide guest shuttles between ceremony venues, reception halls, and hotels. Our coaches comfortably seat up to 54 guests with luxury amenities. We coordinate timing with your wedding planner to ensure seamless transportation throughout the event. Learn more about wedding transportation.

Do you provide transportation for school field trips?

Yes. Werner Coach has been providing school transportation since 1929. Our motorcoaches are USDOT-compliant, and all drivers pass enhanced background checks. We work with schools across the Philadelphia region for field trips, college visits, academic competitions, band and choir travel, and sporting events. Learn more about school transportation.

What amenities are on a Werner Coach bus?

Every motorcoach includes:

  • Reclining seats with headrests
  • Onboard restroom
  • Full climate control (heat and A/C)
  • Complimentary Wi-Fi
  • Power outlets at every seat
  • Overhead storage
  • Undercarriage luggage compartments
  • PA/video entertainment system

See our full fleet details.

How long has Werner Coach been in business?

Werner Coach was established in 1929 in Phoenixville, Pennsylvania. With nearly a century of continuous family ownership and operation, we are one of the oldest charter bus companies in the Philadelphia region.

How do I get a free quote?

We provide complimentary quotes with no obligation. Submit a quote request online or call (800) 532-9800 during business hours. We just need your group size, travel dates, and pickup/destination to provide an accurate price.
