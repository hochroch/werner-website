About Werner Coach

Nearly a century of family ownership and safe, reliable group transportation.

Nearly a Century on the Road

Werner Coach was founded in 1929 in Phoenixville, Pennsylvania. Through the Great Depression, World War II, and every decade since, Werner Coach has remained a family-owned, locally operated motorcoach company serving the Greater Philadelphia region and the entire Northeast corridor.

Today, nearly a century later, we still operate out of Phoenixville with the same commitment to safe, reliable, and comfortable group transportation that built our reputation.

What We Do

Werner Coach provides private luxury motorcoach transportation for groups of all sizes. Whether you need a single coach for a day trip or multiple vehicles for a multi-day event, we handle every detail from routing and scheduling to driver assignment and real-time tracking.

  • Charter Bus Rental: private motorcoach hire for any occasion, single-day or multi-day
  • Wedding Transportation: guest shuttles between ceremony, reception, and hotel venues
  • Corporate Transportation: employee shuttles, conference travel, team outings, executive group transport
  • School & Educational Trips: field trips, college visits, academic competitions, band and choir travel
  • Athletic Team Travel: comfortable transport for high school, college, and club sports teams
  • Custom Tours: single-day and multi-day itineraries built by our in-house trip planning department
  • Special Events: bachelor/bachelorette parties, family reunions, church groups, senior outings

Our Fleet

We operate a fleet of modern, full-size luxury motorcoaches, each seating up to 54 passengers. Every coach is equipped with:

  • Reclining seats with headrests
  • Onboard restroom
  • Full climate control (heat and A/C)
  • Complimentary Wi-Fi
  • Power outlets at every seat
  • Overhead and undercarriage luggage storage
  • PA system and video entertainment
  • Onboard safety camera systems

Our vehicles are USDOT-registered and FMCSA-compliant. Every coach undergoes nightly cleaning, regular preventive maintenance, and DOT-mandated safety inspections.

Our Drivers

Every Werner Coach driver holds a valid Commercial Driver's License (CDL) with a passenger endorsement. All drivers complete:

  • DOT-mandated background checks
  • Pre-employment and random drug and alcohol testing
  • Ongoing safety training and performance reviews
  • Driver Qualification File (DQF) compliance, maintained in full per FMCSA regulations

Many of our drivers have been running the Philadelphia region for years. They bring local road knowledge, venue familiarity, and routing experience that makes a real difference on every trip.

Safety & Compliance

Safety is the foundation of everything we do at Werner Coach. We maintain full compliance with the U.S. Department of Transportation (USDOT) and the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA). Our compliance program includes:

  • Complete Driver Qualification Files for every driver
  • Regular vehicle inspections and preventive maintenance schedules
  • Onboard camera systems on all coaches
  • GPS tracking and real-time fleet monitoring
  • Accident reporting and DOT recordkeeping

Service Area

Werner Coach is headquartered in Phoenixville, Pennsylvania, and primarily serves:

  • Philadelphia and all surrounding suburbs
  • Chester County, Montgomery County, Delaware County, Bucks County
  • King of Prussia, Valley Forge, West Chester, Downingtown
  • The tri-state region: Pennsylvania, New Jersey, and Delaware
  • New York City and northern New Jersey
  • Washington, D.C. and the Baltimore/I-95 corridor

We regularly operate throughout the entire Northeast corridor from New York City to Washington, D.C., and everywhere in between. Atlantic City, the Poconos, Gettysburg, Baltimore, and destinations across the East Coast are all within our standard service range.
