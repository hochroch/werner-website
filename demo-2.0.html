About Werner Coach

A Legacy of Excellence on the Road

Founded in 1929 in Phoenixville, Pennsylvania, Werner Coach has been a cornerstone of the Philadelphia-area transportation industry for nearly a century. What started as a single bus serving the local community has evolved into a fleet of modern, luxury motorcoaches trusted by schools, corporations, wedding planners, and sports teams across the region.

Today, we continue the Werner family tradition of putting passengers first — combining old-fashioned reliability with modern comfort and safety standards. Every trip is an opportunity to earn your trust, and we take that seriously.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my charter bus?
We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance, especially during peak seasons (spring and fall). For weddings and large events, booking 2-3 months ahead ensures you get your preferred date and vehicle.
How many passengers can your buses accommodate?
Our 54-passenger motorcoaches comfortably seat up to 54 passengers with reclining seats, climate control, and ample luggage storage. For larger groups, we can arrange multiple vehicles.
What areas do you serve?
We're based in Phoenixville, PA and primarily serve the greater Philadelphia area, including Chester County, Montgomery County, Delaware County, and Bucks County. We also handle trips throughout the Northeast and beyond.
What is your cancellation policy?
Cancellation policies vary depending on the type and size of your booking. Generally, cancellations made 14+ days before the trip receive a full refund. Please contact us for specific details regarding your reservation.
Are your drivers licensed and insured?
Absolutely. All Werner Coach drivers are DOT-certified, hold valid Commercial Driver's Licenses (CDL), and undergo regular background checks, drug testing, and safety training. Our fleet is fully insured and regularly inspected.
Can I get a quote without committing to a booking?
Of course! Our quotes are completely free and carry no obligation. Simply fill out our quote form or give us a call, and we'll provide a detailed estimate for your trip.
Do you offer wedding transportation packages?
Yes! We specialize in wedding guest transportation and offer dedicated shuttle services between ceremony and reception venues. Our team works directly with wedding planners to coordinate timing and logistics for a seamless experience.
What amenities are available on the bus?
Our motorcoaches feature comfortable reclining seats, overhead storage, climate control, a PA system, and restroom facilities. We can also arrange add-ons like bottled water for your group.
