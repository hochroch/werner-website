Why Werner Coach

A Bus Company,
Not a Broker

You ride on our coaches, driven by our drivers, maintained in our shop. No middlemen, no surprises.

Family-Owned Since 1929

One of the oldest charter bus companies in the Philadelphia region, with nearly a century of continuous family ownership and an unbroken record for reliability.

Direct Fleet Operator

Every coach is company-owned and maintained in our Phoenixville shop. No subcontracting, no unfamiliar drivers, no surprises on the day of your trip.

Safety & Compliance

Full USDOT and FMCSA compliance. Background-checked drivers, onboard cameras, GPS tracking, and regular DOT inspections at every service interval.

Transparent Pricing

One quote covers the coach, driver, fuel, and tolls. No surprise surcharges, no hidden fees, no bait-and-switch pricing on the day you travel.

Local Expertise

Based in Phoenixville with deep knowledge of southeastern Pennsylvania, the tri-state region, and every mile of the I-95 corridor from New York to Washington.

Full-Service Trip Planning

Our team builds custom itineraries and handles all routing, scheduling, and logistics — so you arrive on time without the headache.
