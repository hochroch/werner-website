Wedding Transportation & Shuttle Services

Luxury guest shuttles for weddings throughout the Greater Philadelphia area.

Make Your Wedding Day Seamless

Your wedding day has enough moving parts. Transportation should not be one of them. Werner Coach provides luxury motorcoach shuttle service for weddings throughout the Greater Philadelphia area, ensuring your guests arrive on time, together, and stress-free.

We've been transporting wedding parties since 1929. Whether you're shuttling 20 guests between a church and a reception hall or coordinating multiple coaches across three venues, our team handles the logistics so your day runs smoothly.

How Wedding Shuttle Service Works

  1. Tell us your venues and timeline: ceremony, reception, hotel(s), and any other stops
  2. We build a shuttle schedule coordinated with your wedding planner or coordinator
  3. Our driver handles the rest: on-time pickups, guest loading, and venue-to-venue transport

Most wedding clients book one or two round-trip shuttles:

  • Ceremony to Reception (and return)
  • Hotel to Ceremony to Reception to Hotel

We can also provide dedicated bridal party transport, rehearsal dinner shuttles, or morning-after brunch pickups.

Pricing

Wedding charter pricing depends on the number of hours, distance between venues, and time of year. A typical 4-6 hour wedding shuttle in the Philadelphia area ranges from $1,200 to $2,200 for a single 54-passenger motorcoach. Peak season (May through October) books quickly, so we recommend reserving 2-3 months in advance.

Wedding Transportation FAQ

How early should I book wedding transportation?

We recommend 2-3 months in advance, especially for Saturday weddings during peak season (May through October). Popular dates sell out, so the earlier you book, the better your chances of securing your preferred coach.

Can you do multiple shuttles in one evening?

Yes. We can run continuous loops between your hotel and venues, or provide multiple coaches for simultaneous routes.

What if our timeline changes?

Wedding timelines shift. Our dispatch team stays in contact with your coordinator on the day of the event to adjust as needed.

Is gratuity included?

Driver gratuity is not included in the quoted price and is at your discretion.
